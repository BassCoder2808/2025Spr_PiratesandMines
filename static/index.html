Treasure Hunt Puzzle

Find treasures, avoid landmines, and solve the puzzle using logical deduction!

How to Play

  • Each cell can contain a treasure (+1), a landmine (-1), or be empty (0).
  • Empty cells show clues - the sum of values of cells above, below, left, and right.
  • Use these clues to deduce where treasures and landmines are located.
  • Your goal is to correctly identify all treasures and landmines.
